Carbon Credits Explained

What is a carbon credit?

1 Credit = 1 Tonne of CO₂ reduced or removed

MRV: Measured & Verified

Independent auditors prove every tonne reduced against a baseline.

🪙

Registry issues the credit

Verra / Gold Standard issues one certified credit per verified tonne.
